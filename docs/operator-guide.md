# Operator guide — TON

Portal: no dedicated e-procurement transactional portal was confirmed
operational this session -- `gov.to/procurement` states all Ministries,
Departments and Agencies must procure under the Public Procurement
Regulations 2015 (Public Finance Management Act 2002, s.44), hosted
directly on `finance.gov.to`. Entity: Ministry of Commerce, Consumer,
Trade, Innovation and Labour (MCCTIL) business/company registration
(Companies Act 1995, Business Licences Act 2002) plus Ministry of
Revenue and Customs Tax Identification Number (TIN) record. Foreign-
owned engagements seeking a Foreign Investment Registration Certificate
(FIRC, Foreign Investment Act 2020) must clear the Act's own Reserved/
Restricted-List gate (Foreign Investment Regulations 2021, Schedules
1-2) -- a Reserved-List activity is barred outright for an overseas
person; a Restricted-List activity requires that activity's own
itemized Tongan-equity-percentage/paid-up-capital conditions to be
independently satisfied. Human-gated filing only.
